The Perfect Ribeye!

The perfect ribeye cooked using the oven and iron pan sear method.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye Steak 1 Pound
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Season steak with salt and pepper.
  2. Season Iron pan with Butter.
  3. Sear steak for 2 minutes on both sides then insert into oven at Broil for 10minutes.
  4. Remove from oven and sear both sides of steak for 2 minutes each.
  5. Remove from pan immediately and place inside loose foil, let cool for 5 minutes.
  6. Enjoy!
